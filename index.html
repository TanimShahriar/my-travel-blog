My travel to Turkey

Experience

I was only in Turkey very, very briefly. I was on my way to Kyrgyzstan with a layover in Istanbul and my baggage had accidentally gotten re-routed; this meant I had to purchase a visa to go out and check it through. I had a long layover - about 10 hours, and was with some other people (we were all in the Peace Corps, which is why we were going to Kyrgyzstan). One of my fellow volunteers spoke Turkish, and we decided to head out into the city to check out the Blue Mosque, the Byzantine Walls, and just kind of wander around for a bit.

Visiting Istanbul

Memories

I visited Istanbul for 4.5 days in April 2016. Here are my impressions based on order of events:-

  • Nice weather!
  • Good airport (Ataturk)
  • Comfortable subway ride!
  • Lots of cute and loving elderly couples and they were nice to me, asking me to sit etc
  • Helpful people despite language barrie
